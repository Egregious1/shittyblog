First time for everything
12-7-20

Another go at this silly project to learn how to develop web sites. It seems like such a silly idea that I have been chasing for the better part of a year now and because I am a very lazy individual I cant seem to keep myself motivated enough to keep the nose to the grinding stone as it were.

The thing is I have this dream of living a life where I can work from anywhere. As of right now I work in retail which means a sedintary life for the most part. It makes having freedom of movement really hard to achieve. Dont get me wrong I love my job, I work in an art store and it feels good getting people in the mindset of an artist if they are new to it and helping seasoned artist to find new paths to the answer they are trying to find. Its just a job though. I dont get paid very well in reality to the amount of time I am putting forth and the skills I am learning are only useful to the company that I am working for. its a bit of a drag to be honest.

Part of me thinks it might because I really dont know what I want to do with this skill set. What am I really trying to accomplish? Do I even want to do this at all?

I can at least answer that last question. Yes, I do. I have always been interested in the idea of making web sites. Even as a kid with angelfire and geocities and the myriad of other web site creation platforms. I was not even sure what I was really doing but I made new pages time after time. I never had a good idea as to what I was trying to make. Its something that always vexes me when it comes to creation. I dont have the mindset that takes a concept and can flesh it out in a theoritical sense. I get stuck on go and I don't even know where square one is. Its my own fault I let myself feel defeated but no more. I understand that I am my own worse enemy and I need to make changes if I want to have a better life. This might be the start of something good...
